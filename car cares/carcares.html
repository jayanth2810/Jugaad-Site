CAR CARES:

        Car Cares is a Car washing and Detailing centre that has provided Top Quality Polishing Service to all vehicles from Maruti, Hyundai, Honda, Skoda to Rolls Royce, Ferrari, Lamborghini, etc. We have our centre on the O.M.R. where all polishing and washing services are provided. Working timings are from Monday to Saturday- 9.30am to 6.30pm. We offer services to 200 cars a month on an average and take pride in the Quality of our work. We are currently looking for freshers looking to gain Experience in the fields of Marketing, Customer Relations, Workforce Efficiency Management, Professional Communication Skills and Work Ethic. Educational background and Qualification will add knowledge to the Student but is not a selection criteria of ours for Freshers. We are looking for those freshers who would like 6 months to 1 year's experience and are willing to work hard with dedication and honesty. The Experience provided in our company is more than just learning how to wash and polish a car, we intend on imparting and inculcating lessons by which Employees can work efficiently and add value to the company. Fulltime Work and Intern-ships are offered at our company. Salaries offered will be based on Job Description. After 2months, it will be increased based on Performance and Result Delivered each month by each individual.
