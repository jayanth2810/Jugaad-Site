JUGAAD

For quite some time, an entrepreneurial wave had been sweeping the Anna university campus. This unbridled energy and a fierce spirit of entrepreneurship among its students gave birth to the idea of an Entrepreneurship Development Club(EDC) at Anna university. The EDC at Anna university was established under the auspices of the Centre for entrepreneurship development comprising of students from CEG,ACT,SAP and DOM.
In 2010, the entire team got together and set the tone for things to follow, by rechristening the club as “JUGAAD”. The word symbolically captures the entrepreneurial attitude of “looking for a way around” for all the problems.

A Company’s most valuable asset is its human resource.
There are plenty of smart,hardworking people out there but the people you need to take a long bumpy ride are those who can face real world problems. The ultimate challenge lies in choosing the right person for the right job.
In many cases, the best candidates on paper may not be the best fit for a new business. Here passion supersedes skill-set. ‘Connect!’ is an exclusive platform where it conjoins the 21st century revolutionists- The entrepreneurs and the torch-bearers of tomorrow- the students. It’s a unique opportunity provided for all the students, where they get a face-off with the company’s representatives, a chance to express their talents with no bars on their academics. When you find the right person. Set the sails of your ship right, catch the trade winds and sail away for the safe harbor.
