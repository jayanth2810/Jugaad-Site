ABOUT CONNECT!
A Company’s most valuable asset is its human resource.
There are plenty of smart,hardworking people out there but the people you need to take a long bumpy ride are those who can face real world problems. The ultimate challenge lies in choosing the right person for the right job.
In many cases, the best candidates on paper may not be the best fit for a new business. Here passion supersedes skill-set.
‘Connect!’ is an exclusive platform where it conjoins the 21st century revolutionists- The entrepreneurs and the torch-bearers of tomorrow- the students. It is a unique opportunity provided for all the students, where they get a face-off with the company’s representatives, a chance to express their talents with no bars on their academics. When you find the right person. Set the sails of your ship right, catch the trade winds and sail away for the safe harbor.

EVENT FORMAT:
1.The participants are expected to register online and fill their resume.
2.The participants are expected to arrive at the venue by 8 AM with 3 copies of their resume.
3.There will be a check-in for online registered participants.
4.There will be a full registration process for non-registered participants wherein they will have to fill their resume on the spot, as per our format and print 3 copies.
5.The registration fee will be 200 INR for both online and on-site registration.
6.Each participant can choose a maximum of 3 companies to interact with. The participant can specify his/her choices at the time of registration or after attending the companies? presentation. However, it is not possible to modify your choices at any point in the event.
7.The registrations will close by 9:15 and the event will be inaugurated, following which the companies will start their presentations.
8.After the presentation, the participants can interact with the startups as per a schedule that we provide.
9.Each participant will be given a fixed time slot to interact with each of the 3 companies that he/she chooses. We shall not assure any form of adjustment/compensation in the event of missing the allotted time slot.
10.Jugaad shall not provide any information to contact the startups after the event. The participant must collect all the required information within his/her interaction.
11.The event will end by 7 PM. Some startups might choose to leave early. In case we?re not able to fix an interaction slot for a few participants due to lack of time, we shall send your resume and contact details to your desired companies. We shall ensure that they correspond with you after the event over phone, skype or mail.

JUGAAD:

Jugaad is the Entrepreneurship Development Club (EDC) of Anna University. The club was formed to garner the entrepreneurial talent among the students of the Anna varsity campus. A fierce spirit for entrepreneurship and management gave birth to the club in the year 2008.
The EDC at Anna University was established by the Centre for Entrepreneurship Development and the students from CEG, ACT, SAP and DoMs. The aim of the club is to channelize and focus the entrepreneurial talents of the students. The club was rechristened as "JUGAAD" in the year 2011. The word symbolizes the entrepreneurial attitude of "looking for a way around" for all the problems
